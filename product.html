OUR PRODUCTS

We offer different cuts of chilled and frozen beef, veal, and lamb from some of the most reputable farms and suppliers around the world. We work with partners in countries like Australia, Argentina, Brazil, Paraguay, South Africa, and various European countries.

We carry a wide range of ingredients for industrial and artisanal bakers, pastry shops and industrial manufacturers alike. We work with some of the leading global manufacturers of specialty ingredients who have a decades old track record of being at the forefront of new product development, and we also bring our team’s long years of experience to locally manufacture ingredients, under very strict quality standards, to be consumed in both the local & export markets

Products we carry fall within bread mixes & decorations, bakery premixes & improvers, pastry fillings & topings, cake spreads, whipping cream (liquid & powder) and chocolate (real & compound).

We work hard to present unique products that serve the retail segment and would add significant value to the retail channels in Egypt.

We serve the retail market by distributing a variety of high end, imported fresh meat products that are sold on meat counters across the nation. We also used our experience to carefully craft & design a high value, competitively priced, frozen meat product range, Moomoo’s. We started with our premium South African Angus Veal product, and plan to roll out more products soon.

Our product range:

Fillet Steak - Striploin Steak - Ribeye Steak - Burgers - Sausages - Oven Kofta Breaded Escalope - Minced Meat - Meat Cubes - Meat Balls - Fillet Strips - Liver Slices - Liver Strips
